Exam Rules

Please read the following rules carefully before starting the exam:

  • Ensure a stable internet connection during the entire exam.
  • Do not refresh or close the browser while the exam is in progress.
  • Any attempt to switch tabs or minimize the window may lead to disqualification.
  • Keep your camera on (if required) for proctoring purposes.
  • Make sure to submit your answers before the timer runs out.
  • No external help, notes, or devices are allowed during the exam.
  • Do not use the "Back" button of your browser during the test.
  • Once the test starts, it cannot be paused or restarted.
